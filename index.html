About Higher Market

Higher Market is a creative platform that connects makers, artists, and innovators in the Higher ecosystem. We're building a community where creativity meets collaboration, where unique projects find their audience, and where the spirit of making drives everything we do.

Our Mission

To create a space where independent creators can showcase their work, connect with like-minded individuals, and contribute to the Higher community's creative output.

What is Higher?

Higher is a decentralized creative community focused on elevating art, culture, and human potential. We believe in the power of creative expression to drive positive change and meaningful connections.

Values

  • Creative excellence and authenticity
  • Community-driven collaboration
  • Supporting independent makers
  • Innovation in digital culture
  • Sustainable creative practices
